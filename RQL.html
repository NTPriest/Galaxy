Inverse Square Law

The farther you are from a signal (like Wi-Fi), the weaker it gets — fast. Not just double weak at double distance, but 4 times weaker. Move 3x away? It’s 9x weaker.

TL;DR: Distance kills signal — quick. Stay close to your router!
