About

~everything~

- This isn't just a website — it's a little galaxy -


I’m building, one page, one tool, one idea at a time. It's a place for learning, tinkering, sharing, and growing — for both myself and hopefully anyone who stumbles in.

I’ve been around tech for years, working in IT, fixing things, exploring systems. But it wasn’t until recently that I realized what I really wanted to do:


- build something lasting -

- something useful -

- aesthetic -

- and open -

Something that feels like it belongs to the web of the past, but with the soul of now.

Whether you're here out of curiosity, need a quick tool, or just enjoy the DIY vibe — welcome. This space is for you. And yeah, I know the world can be a mess, but maybe this little corner can be something good. Something helpful. Something real.

No flashy frameworks, no corporate polish. Just hand-built pages, heart-coded. Static for now — because simplicity is security — but always growing.

One day I might move to a full backend, with a CMS and even deeper tools (nmap online, nslookup, ping, whois etc.).

I don't expect to change the world. But if this site makes your day easier, teaches you something new, or inspires you to build something of your own — then it's done its job.

You're in my galaxy now. Stay a while.
